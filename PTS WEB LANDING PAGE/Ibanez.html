Ibanez

TOD10

Electric Guitar

The Ibanez TOD10 Tim Henson is a signature model guitar designed in collaboration with Tim Henson, a guitarist known for his work in the band Polyphia. This guitar features a unique and visually striking design, with a semi-hollow body and a distinctive "Tree of Life" inlay on the fretboard. It's equipped with high-quality pickups and hardware, making it suitable for a wide range of musical styles, especially for players who enjoy a fusion of modern and traditional elements in their music. The TOD10 is well-regarded for its playability and versatility, making it a popular choice among guitarists seeking a unique instrument with a modern edge.

Biography

Tim Henson

Tim Henson is a renowned guitarist, songwriter, and producer known for his innovative contributions to the world of instrumental music. He was born on December 2, 1991, in Los Angeles, California, USA. Tim gained widespread recognition as a founding member of the progressive rock band Polyphia. From a young age, Tim exhibited a deep passion for music and began playing the guitar. His extraordinary talent and dedication to his craft quickly set him apart in the music scene. Alongside fellow guitarist Scott LePage, Tim co-founded Polyphia in 2010. The band's unique blend of progressive rock, metal, and elements of various other genres captivated audiences around the world. One of Tim Henson's notable collaborations includes his partnership with Ibanez, a renowned guitar manufacturer. The Ibanez TOD10 Tim Henson signature guitar, designed in collaboration with Tim, reflects his unique style and preferences, featuring a distinctive design and high-quality components. Beyond his musical endeavors, Tim Henson's influence extends to the realm of guitar education and YouTube. He has shared his knowledge and expertise with aspiring guitarists through tutorials, gear reviews, and insightful videos on his YouTube channel, further cementing his reputation as a prominent figure in the guitar community.

Features

Oval C Roasted Maple neck

The Roasted Maple neck is heat-treated to increase stability, durability, moisture resistance and tolerance of temperature changes.The Graphite reinforcement rods provides massive sustain and excellent stability.

Ebony fretboard

Ebony produces tight lows and mid range with a strong attack in the high end and features a quick response and rich sustain.

American Basswood body

The American Basswood body delivers a natural and flat tone.

Fishman® Fluence™ Tim Henson Signature Series pickups

Tim's signature Fishman® Fluence™ pickups span the entire range of aggressive lead and rhythm tones to the cleanest of clean single coil combinations, even touching on sounds Tim was previously only able to produce with his acoustic guitars. Neck Pickup Voice 1: Thick and smooth neck humbucker / Voice 2: Hyper-clean, nylon string surrogate/ Voice 3: Tim's fluid neck single coilBridge. Pickup Voice 1: Based on Fishman®'s hot-rodded Classic voice, the quintessential lead tone of generation / Voice 2: Combination of Tim's favorite bridge humbuckers / Voice 3: Tim's ideal single coil combination tone when used in conjunction with neck coil.

Gotoh® T1502 tremolo

The T1502 tremolo bridge features steel saddles and a die-cast zinc tremolo block for a quick response and improved articulation. The 10.5mm string spacing allows for easy picking across strings and its snap-in construction makes it easy to load a tremolo arm.

Fishman® Fluence™ Voicing switch on tone control (push/pull) for Tim Henson Signature Series pickups

The Voicing switch allows the pickups to switch between three musically complementary voices.
